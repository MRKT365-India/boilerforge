# Teams Beta

Boilerforge Teams Beta is for engineering teams that want governed scaffolding workflows and predictable upgrades.

## Paid Features

1. Private Boilerplate Registry
Your team can host internal templates and expose them through the same MCP workflow used for public boilerplates.

2. Policy & Compliance Controls
Enforce required files, dependency policies, and metadata checks before a template can be scaffolded.

3. Update Management Dashboard
Track project lockfile versions across repos and surface which projects are behind the latest approved boilerplate.

## Join the Beta

Email `team@cometforge.dev` with subject `Boilerforge Teams Beta` and include your org name, team size, and current stack.
